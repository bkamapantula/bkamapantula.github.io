About

Bhanu is a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science, VCU. He explores the structural robustness in biological and complex networks with applications to wireless sensor networks. Primary objective of the research is to identify features responsible for biological robustness from a structural perspective.

His interests are in data science, data journalism, data visualization, network science and systems biology. Research portfolio displays several of his research projects and publication details. Visualization portfolio mentions several interactive and static visuals developed by him.
