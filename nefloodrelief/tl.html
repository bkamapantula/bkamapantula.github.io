Timeline of #NEFloodRelief
Recent floods in Northeast India created havoc in the daily life of people in parts of Assam and Meghalaya. Many volunteers online and on-ground have teamed up to coordinate relief work. The following timeline presents day by day developments of the relief effort. The information used is adapted from internal emails and incrisisrelief.org blog updates.
