#letsdomore

The low numbers of women representation in Indian elected bodies must increase. #letsdomore is an effort to raise awareness on the improper representation of women in parliament and state assemblies. All 29 states in India have less than 15% MLAs in respective assemblies which is not at all representative of the population.
